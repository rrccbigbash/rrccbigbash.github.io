player of the match

Saturday Match
85
Kamlesh Malviya

When your back is up against the wall, you look for game changers to put their hand up. On a day when 3 halves of the match belonged to Dragons, Kamlesh with some strong team support turned the tide around in the last half, taking three crucial wickets at important turning points to lead BB to a well earned charismatic win. Kamlesh, first sent a dangerous well set Sampath to cleaners and then followed by wickets of other two batting stalwarts of RRCC, one being in good form Mohit and other the ever dependable Rama.

He bowled a taut probing line drying up the runs, which not only created pressure on batsman but also forced them to play run manufacturing shots off wicket taking bowls, that eventually lead to their downfall. Earlier in the first innings as usual he made quick fire runs to help push up a respectable score that came in handy while defending. A well deserved MOM for coming to a timely rescue for his team!
