About the club

It’s not just like that they say cricket can unite a bunch of Indians anywhere. More so, when they are living as expats in a home away from home. RRCC is a true testimonial to that.

What started more than 8 years back, as a quintessential backyard void-deck cricket-like something between 4-5 cricket crazy guys, has now swollen into a rather big social club swarming with enthusiastic members of varying degrees of, what can only be aptly termed as “cricket-giri”.

What started as just another ‘time pass” has now snowballed into a something more serious than just time pass… “A serious time pass” if you must.
